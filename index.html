Logo Stress Test

This tool is designed to help you identify potential weaknesses in your design. Is your logo legible at a small scale? Is the shape interesting even when it's blurry? Does it work in a single color application? Like a good designer, a good logo design should be versatile and resilient, holding up under adverse conditions.
